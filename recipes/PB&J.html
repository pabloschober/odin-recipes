Pablo's Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwitch

Image here

This is the most delicious pb&j known to man. Feed this to your kids, grandkids, friends, or literally anyone... they're going to love it.

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Stwarberry jelly
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Get two slices of sourdough bread
  2. On one side of the bread, smoothly spread out some peanut butter.
  3. On the other slice of bread, do the same but with the jelly
  4. Now, drizzle a substantial amount of honey on the bread with PEANUT BUTTER
  5. You're welcome
