This is a Pablo classic that never fails to have guests quick to ask for seconds and eager to know the recipe. The secret behind this incredible dish lies in the sauce. Getting the sauce perfect is absolutely essential to nailing the flavor of this dish. Above all, make sure you follow the steps closely.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Heirloom Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Spicy Sausage
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Cream

Instructions:

  1. Lay out all your ingredients, a cutting board, a medium-sized pot, and two medium-sized pans
  2. Fill the pot with water and begin to boil
  3. Pour a little bit of olive oil and butter on both pans and begin to heat
  4. While water is boiling and pans are heating up, dice your onions and throw them on pan 1
  5. Cut your heirloom tomatoes and throw this in with the onions
  6. Cut up your spicy sausage into small chunks and throw them in pan 2
  7. Salt your onions and tomatoes and your spicy sausage
  8. When the water starts to boil, throw your spaghetti in there
  9. Throw some tomatoe paste into pan 1 along with some cream
  10. Follow that up with basil
  11. Now some parmesan cheese
  12. Mix the spicy sausage into pan 1
  13. Now toss the cooked spaghetti in there too
  14. More parmesan!
